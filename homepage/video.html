Iceland Volcano Erupts Overnight:
Key Moments and Updates

Video info:

ERUPTION ON THE REYKJANES PENINSULA Around 9pm in Iceland, a volcanic eruption has commenced on the Reykjanes Peninsula, triggered by a powerful earthquakes. Lava is flowing. The ongoing eruption between Sýlingarfell and Hagafell in Iceland, is concentrated in the magma tunnel north of Grindavík. Welcome to this brief update on the eruption.
